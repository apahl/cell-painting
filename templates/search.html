SUBPROFILE EXPLORER


This tool provides access to the bioactivity cluster similarity for 1883 reference (landmark) compounds as determined using a subprofiles derived from Cell painting analysis in U2OS cells upon compound treatment for 20h. Thus far, ten clusters were defined that are related to modulation of AKT/PI3K/MTOR, Aurora kinases, BET, DNA synthesis, HDAC, HSP90 and tubulin or processes like lysosomotropism/cholesterol homeostasis regulation (L/CH), protein synthesis and uncoupling of the mitochondrial proton gradient.
